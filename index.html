BeeSpelled is an Android application that provides an easy way for students to study for spelling bees and tests. Users are able to create their own word lists to study, making it easy for them to study anywhere and at any time!


What makes BeeSpelled better than other methods of studying?

  • Users get a real spelling bee experience by being able to spell words out loud as a form of answering
  • Users can hear the words, origins, definitions, and sentences out loud in a clear, understandable voice
  • Users get a fun way to study with the easy to use quiz system
